# Demo GIF Instructions

1.  **Preparation**
    - Open `http://localhost:3000` in Chrome.
    - Have `packshot_cereal.png` ready on Desktop/Folder.
    - Set window size to ~1280x800 for clean aspect ratio.

2.  **Recording Steps (20-30s)**
    - **Step 1 (0s-5s)**: Upload image. Show it appear. Click "Remove BG".
    - **Step 2 (5s-10s)**: Select "1080x1920" from dropdown. Click "Suggest Layouts". Click one layout.
    - **Step 3 (10s-15s)**: Click on "Headline". Type "Best Cereal".
    - **Step 4 (15s-20s)**: Click "Validate". Show Green Pass result.
    - **Step 5 (20s-22s)**: Click "Export". Show "Download" link appear.

3.  **Capture Tool**
    - Use LICEcap or ScreenToGif.
    - Save as `demo.gif`.
